Zodiac Memory Matching Game

Welcome to the Zodiac Memory Matching Game! The goal of the game is to match all the zodiac signs. Click on a card to flip it over and reveal the zodiac sign. Click on another card to find its match. If the two cards match, they will stay flipped over. If the two cards do not match, they will flip back over. Keep playing until you have matched all the zodiac signs. Good luck!
